¿What is a Digital Footprint?

A Digital Footprint is the detectable data and activity a user leaves on the internet. You add information to your digital footprint when visit a wbside, send an email, submit an online form, or do any otrher traceable activity online

¿How to take care of your digital footprint?

You can take care of your Digital Footprint following these measures: Browse the internet in incognito mode to avoid cookies Deleted old accounts Use tighter privacy settings Protecting yourself online Update your passwords online monthly
